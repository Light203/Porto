Lip Reading Application for Investigative Analysis

Developed a Lip-Reading Application for Investigative Analysis utilizing LSTM neural networks. The application analyzes sequences of video frames to interpret lip movements and predict spoken words.

  • Lip reading with LSTM networks finds applications in speech recognition, assistive technologies, and surveillance systems.
  • Achieved an accuracy of 93.23% on the LRW Dataset.
  • Led a team of 4 members to successful project completion.
  • Technologies Used: Streamlit, Keras, TensorFlow, LSTM, Convo3d.
